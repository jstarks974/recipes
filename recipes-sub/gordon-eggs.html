Gordon Ramsay Scrambled Eggs

Description

Creamy scrambled eggs cooked to perfection using Gordon Ramsay's signature method.

Ingredients

  • 6 cold eggs
  • 1.5 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon creme fraiche
  • Chopped chives to garnish

Equipment

  • A deep saucepan
  • Rubber spatula

Directions

  1. Crack the eggs into your unheated saucepan.
  2. Add butter.
  3. Turn the head to medium-high.
  4. Stir continuously with a rubber spatula, making sure to scrape the bottom.
  5. After 30 seconds, take the pan off the heat. Continue stirring for 10 seconds. Place pan back on the heat. Repeat for 3 minutes or until eggs are done.
  6. Off the heat, season lightly with salt and pepper.
  7. Stir in 1 teaspoon of creme fraiche.
  8. Plate and garnish with chives.
